Mustard Tofu recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cubes of tofu, each 250 g
  • 1/2 cup mustard, table or sarepska
  • 1/4 cup of dark beer
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 onion, cut into eighths

Preparation:

  1. Drain the tofu cubes from the brine and cut lengthwise
    into three slices - so as to obtain thin, oblong, rectangular
    pieces.
  2. Mix all marinade ingredients in a jar. Then pour a few
    tablespoons of marinade into the bottom of the rectangular
    container, arrange the slices of tofu, pour a few tablespoons
    of marinade over them again, put the last pieces of tofu on
    top and pour out the rest of the marinade. Cover with a large
    piece of foil, and put a jar of jam or other preserves on it -
    thanks to the weight, the tofu will marinate better.
  3. Leave in the fridge for the whole night. The next day, grill on
    the grill, basting with the remaining marinade if necessary.
    Sprinkle with black pepper, serve with bread and your favorite
    toppings.

Tips:

  • If your tofu cubes are smaller, you can prepare the same
    amount of marinade and add a few mushrooms or zucchini
    slices to the tofu.
  • Both natural and smoked tofu will work well in this recipe.
  • You can serve them in the form of a large steak, or cut them
    into cubes and put them on skewers.
  • Instead of beer, you can pour natural apple or pear juice into
    the marinade.
